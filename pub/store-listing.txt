Full description:
This app adds Banff Roam Transit buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the buses schedule as well as the latest news from @RoamTransit on Twitter.

Roam Transit buses serve Banff and Canmore in the Bow Valley in Alberta, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Town of Banff.
http://www.banffopendata.ca/

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-banff-roam-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Roam Public Transit and the Bow Valley Regional Transit Services Commission.
